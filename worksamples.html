About Blob Bags:
Blob Bags are reusable tote bags with a special focus on marketing and design. It's made for young consumers who want to keep their carbon footprint as minimal as possible. Every bag has an individually designed pattern consisting of unique blob shapes. The strategically placed and easy-to-use vending machine gets the bag in the hands of the consumer, right when he needs it.
